Work Experience

Anmol Nayak

Introduction

During my time in the Engineering Academy, I had the opportunity to listen to many people and travel to many places. These experiences allowed me to gain ideas and experience on what I could do and be in the future. Here are four major instances where I got valuable experience.

Guest Speaker: Alex Tomaz

2021 - 2022 School Year

Alex Tomaz is a guest speaker that educated us on the system that colleges use in order to determine applicants. She told us the process of how they work together with other admission officers in order to carefully select students for their school. As an admission officer for UC Berkeley, she showed us some of the objectives they try to follow in order to have the best student body

Guest Speaker: Donald James

2021 - 2022 School Year

Donald James was appointed as the Associate Administrator for Education at NASA. He told us about his journey and about his loss of his teacher Christa McAuliffe during the Challenger accident. He came to our school to explain his journey through school and as a NASA employee, learning and growing as a person. He also authored the book, Manners Will Take You Where Brains and Money Won't.

Guest Speaker: Gary Richter

2021 - 2022 School Year

Gary Richter was in the field of System Analysis and worked at Sandia Labs. He also worked as a counter terrorists where he would analyze the plans of terrorist threat and report if they were making dangerous objects. He also dismantled nuclear weapons in accordance to U.S policies.

2020 - 2021 School Year

Field Trip: UC Berkeley

2022 - 2023 School Year

I had the opportunity to go to a UC Berkeley tour hosted by the Las Positas College. Here I was able to see how the school looked and felt. We visited throughout the campus and visited an classroom and saw how the class still used chalk and chalkboard. I also had the chance to visit the city of Berkeley to see if it would be nice to live in a place like this.

Field Trip: Lawerence Livermore National Labs

2021 - 2022 School Year

During my Junior year, I attened a virtual field trip that allowed me to interact with the people who work at Lawerence Livermore National Lab. I was able to see that type of people that work there as well their Sierra computer, which is an extremely powerful super computer. They have a three billion dollar budget in order to experiment freely as they want.

Field Trip: Dig Deep Farms

2021 - 2022 School Year

When I went to the Dig Deep Farm in San Leandro, I was able to have a greater education in the environmental sciences that they use in order to operate a clean and sustainable farm. They showed us how they were able to survive without using GMOs and sell completely organic plants. They also prided themselves with their ability to use and reuse the waste into clean fertilizer.

Field Trip: San Jose State University

2020 - 2021 School Year

I went to the the Cal State school, San Jose State University, in order to see one of the top universities in the Cal State program. Here I was able to see the great campus and all of the facilities that made this place a perfect place to educate oneself. I also learned how many people get jobs here because of how the college is placed directly in the middle of the Silicon Valley.
